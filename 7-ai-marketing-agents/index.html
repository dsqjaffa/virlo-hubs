One-time keyword search across TikTok, Reels, and YouTube Shorts. Returns videos sorted by views, engagement, and outlier ratios. Includes AI analysis of patterns, recurring hooks, and content formats driving performance.

Content Agents (Comets)
GET /v1/comet/{id}/videos

Scheduled tracking of your market. Auto-refreshes continuously. Surfaces outlier content the moment it appears. Your agents get a live feed of what is winning in your competitive landscape without you manually searching.

Feeds these skills:
/social-content /marketing-ideas /churn-prevention /email-sequence
03
Outlier Detection
Built into Orbit + Comet results

Not biggest videos. Videos that beat their own creator baseline by 2x or more. Relative performance is the real signal. A video with 50K views from a 500-follower account matters more than a 2M-view video from someone with 10M followers.

Feeds these skills:
/page-cro /ab-test-setup /marketing-psychology /cold-email

Orbit with enable_meta_ads: true

Shows which ad creatives are actively spending and converting. Which hooks are winning. Which angles competitors are scaling. Not what looks good. What is actually getting budget allocated to it right now.

Feeds these skills:
